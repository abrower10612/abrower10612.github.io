PARC: Alignment

Pluralsight

www.pluralsight.com

Pluralsight's website is a great example of the use of alignment as they have the text and call to action buttons all aligned left. Also, in the header, they have their logo left-aligned and to create a balance, they have their search and menu icons right-aligned. The audience is able to follow a natural alignment pathway as they view Pluralsight's website content.

Visual Hierarchy

BambooHR

www.bamboohr.com

BambooHR's website shows an excellent example of visual hierarchy as your eyes are naturally drawn to the main header in the middle of the screen. From there, the hierarchy leads you to examine the call to action buttons, and then possibly backtrack to read the subtitle, ultimately guiding their audience with visual hierarchy.

White Space

Thinkific

www.thinkific.com

Thinkific's website is a beautiful example of how powerful white space can be when utilized correctly. They have simple, powerful statements orgaized neatly across the mobile layout and the space in between provides a simple, minimal feeling that keeps the audience content and engaged.
